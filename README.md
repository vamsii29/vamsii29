## About Me

I believe **privacy is a fundamental human right** — and that most of the technology built to protect it is either inaccessible, underdeveloped, or ignored by the mainstream. That belief is what drove me into **cybersecurity and blockchain**.

Blockchain, to me, is one of the most underutilised technologies of our time. My long-term goal is to build **financial infrastructure on blockchain** — systems that are secure, transparent, and don't require people to surrender their privacy to use them.

I'm building the technical foundation deliberately — working across authentication security, security tooling, blockchain core infrastructure, and crypto payment processing.
